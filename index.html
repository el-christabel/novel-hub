Our Story

How NovelHub was born

📖

From Passion to Platform

NovelHub was born from a simple frustration. In 2022, our founder—a passionate reader and indie author—struggled to find a platform that truly understood novel lovers. Existing marketplaces were cluttered, confusing, and didn't celebrate the stories that deserved celebration.

So we built NovelHub from the ground up with one mission: Make it easy for amazing authors to reach eager readers. No distractions. No complexity. Just great stories at fair prices.

Today, NovelHub has become home to thousands of dedicated readers and hundreds of talented authors. We've proudly facilitated over 50,000+ book sales, and our community continues to grow every day.
